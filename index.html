Our Mission

In the UK we throw out 1/3 of our food. In catering, it's more. As a catering company, we want to change this by making sure no food goes to waste.

'How It Works...'

If we any food left over from our client, we bag it up and take it to a nearby location. You'll get a notification if you're within our vicinity and then you can come and purchase some top quality food for very little. All money goes to the charity, Shelter.
